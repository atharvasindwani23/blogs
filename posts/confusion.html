Confusion

Not the bad kind. The kind that means you're actually engaging with something real instead of coasting on what you already know.

So here's a running list of things I'm confused about:

  • How do elevators work?
  • How are sticky notes both adhesive and cohesive?
  • How do electronic key-cards work?
  • What really makes something aesthetic?
  • How did the Egyptians build the pyramids?
  • Are the people on tech Twitter real?
  • How do you decide the trade-off between being scrappy and diving deep?
  • Why are whiteboards so awesome?
  • Can we ever talk to animals?
  • How will the world evolve 10 years from now?
  • What should I spend my time in college doing?
  • If we do good things to feel good about ourselves, aren't we intrinsically selfish?
  • How do you stop feeling cringe about posting online?
